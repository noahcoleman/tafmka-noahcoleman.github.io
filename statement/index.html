Artist Statement

I create immersive experiences that quietly demand to be explored.  Combining simple gestures with robotics, found materials, custom electronics, and glow in the dark paint, I create spaces that typify the nebulous areas between play, experience, ritual, and attention.  This liminal space is rife with nuance and paradox.  I seek to draw the viewer into this space, allowing them to explore it, examine details, and make connections as they navigate the in-between.
